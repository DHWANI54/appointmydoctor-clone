About Appointmydoctor

We're on a mission to democratize healthcare access through innovative technology solutions that connect and empower everyone in the healthcare ecosystem.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Appointmydoctor began with a simple truth. Healthcare in India can be better: more caring, connected, and clear for everyone stakeholders.

Our founders, driven by a shared mission, united their expertise in healthcare, technology, and policy to bridge the gaps that leave patients and doctors struggling.

We're on a heartfelt journey, working closely with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission and striving for ABDM compliance, to create a future where digital health truly empowers every individual, doctor, and community.

Mission

To empower early disease detection through seamless, secure health records helping prevent illness and giving patients and providers the technology they need to care smarter and live healthier.

Vision

A world where everyone, everywhere, has access to quality healthcare where technology connects hearts and minds to transform lives before health issues take hold.

Impact

Bridging India's digital healthcare gap by delivering compassionate, cutting edge technology that reaches every community, enabling healthier futures for all.

The Problem We're Solving

India's healthcare system holds immense promise, yet millions still face barriers that technology can help overcome.

Fragmented Care

Too often, patient stories and vital health data are scattered across different places, leaving doctors without the full picture. This fragmentation leads to missed connections, delayed treatments, and unnecessary worry for both patients and their families.

Access Barriers

For millions living in remote or underserved areas, quality healthcare feels out of reach. Distance, cost, and lack of resources create walls that separate people from the care they deserve.

Data Silos

When health systems don't communicate, lives can be lost in the gaps. Repeated tests, overlooked warnings, and missed diagnoses happen all because crucial information is trapped and isolated.
